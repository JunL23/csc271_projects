Product Features

  • Our product is a schedule planning app that provides services that helps students to save time on their course planning. And offeres many features such as generating course schedules and notifications of class openings to help students to have an easier time to do these processes.


Who we are & why you should use our app

Who we are: I am a college students that has faces the problem of spending too much time on schedule planning and wanted to change that.

Why you should invest in our app: Our product combines many features such as generating schedules and notify of class openings to help students to efficiently manage their academic schedules. This all in one app will bring you a great experience.


Inspiration

  • Our product focuses on solving the problem of students spending a lot of times on different apps to planned and make their schedules. Our product will combine many features into one app to provide students with a smooth and quick experience of course planning for their academic journey.
